Below is a Business Requirements Document (BRD) for building a dynamic website + patient portal + admin panel for the Ayurvedic clinic based on the reference site:
https://www.ayurmantra.in/

The clinic provides Ayurvedic treatments, detox programs, herbal products, skin care treatments, and wellness services.
The new platform should support content management, patient engagement, appointment booking, and product/service management.

Business Requirement Document (BRD)
Project: AyurMantra Digital Platform

Dynamic Website + Patient Portal + Admin Panel

1. Project Overview
1.1 Objective

Develop a modern, scalable digital platform for AyurMantra clinic that includes:

Public Website (End User Website)

Patient Portal (Patient Login)

Admin Panel (Clinic Staff & Owner)

The system must allow administrators to manage website content, services, treatments, products, media, blogs, appointments, and patient records dynamically without developer intervention.

2. Business Goals
Primary Goals

Improve online visibility and patient engagement

Enable easy appointment booking

Provide digital health records for patients

Enable clinic staff to manage content and offerings

Sell herbal products online (optional phase)

Secondary Goals

SEO optimized website

Lead generation and patient conversion

Digital clinic management

3. Stakeholders
Stakeholder	Role
Clinic Owner	Business decisions, service management
Clinic Staff	Appointment management, patient records
Patients	Book appointments, view reports
Admin	Manage website content and operations
Developers	Build and maintain system
